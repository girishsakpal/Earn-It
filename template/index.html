EarnIt: Salary Predictor

About EarnIt

EarnIt predicts salary ranges using ML models trained on developer & industry salary data. It's useful for career planning, negotiations, and benchmarking yourself vs peers.

Why use this?

  • Fast salary range estimate in realistic ranges.
  • Compare yourself to average salaries by profession.
  • Clear visualizations: average by profession, and salary vs experience curves.

Algorithms Tested

  • Linear Regression
  • Random Forest Regressor
  • XGBoost / Gradient Boosting
  • Ensemble (stacking) for final prediction

Algorithm Used

✅ Best Model: Gradient Boosting (R²=0.9352)

Quick Tip

Higher education, certifications, and targeted skills often boost salary more than a single year of experience.
